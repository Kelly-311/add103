Intro to Genshin Impact

The world of Genshin Impact is vast and has rich environments that all tell a story. Some environments are rich with greenery or have a lack of it, places filled with people living, and places that are abandoned for one reason or another. Every environment tells a different story; some are more complex, and others are simpler. Regardless of whether they are complex or simple, they are all unique in their own ways. This is important because each environment has its own story and can allow the player to feel more immersed in the world. On this website, some environments will be briefly explored, so be warned, there are some spoilers ahead. I would also like to disclose, for those who might care, that I do not engage with the larger fandom and, as such, do not agree with the negative actions they sometimes partake in.
