Genshin Impact and the 6.0 the Nod Krai update

Genshin impact is a free to play open-world gacha game that has lots of exploration, unique combat mechanics, and rich story. In less than a week it will enter version 6.0. 6.0 will introduce a new region, new characters, and a new game mode. As well as the games 5th anniversary. Another fun feature to be added in 6.0 is a UGC, User Generated Content, game mode.

Nod Krai

Nod Krai is the new region being added, the main inspiration for this region is countries from the Baltic Sea and Celtic Mythology. The story of Nod Krai is set up to be grand in nature with 11 different factions present and converging in the story, some of the main ones being the Fatui, the Frost Moon Scions, The Knights of Favonius, Adventurers Guild, and more. One of the central characters of Nod Krai will be Colombina also known as Damselette, is the Moon Maiden, who will serve as one the main drivers for the plot and while mystery around her is still present, we will undoubtedly learn more about her as the story progresses.
